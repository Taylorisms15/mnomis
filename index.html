How it works

From behavioral signal to embedded guidance

01

The behavioral patterns are already in your data

Response behavior, care adherence patterns, and channel engagement history are signals already in your systems. Your outreach strategy is just not interpreting them.

02

Patterns become posture

We read those signals across time to classify motivational posture: the durable orientation toward care that persists across interactions, and the situational shifts that occur under stress or at high-stakes moments.

03

Posture becomes action

Channel, tone, timing, and escalation guidance lands directly in the workflow your team is already using. No translation step. No new tool.

The best care managers already do this. They read behavioral cues, adjust their approach, and know when to push and when to wait.

Mnomis makes that instinct available to every care manager on every member — not just the experienced ones, not just on their best days.

What we do differently

Classify motivational posture,
not just clinical risk.

Risk tells you who is vulnerable. Posture tells you how they decide.

Under stress, posture compresses. Readiness is how that compression expresses itself in the moment.

Mnomis interprets structural posture and situational expression to determine how to engage at high-stakes decision points.

Guidance is embedded directly into existing care workflows and systems.
